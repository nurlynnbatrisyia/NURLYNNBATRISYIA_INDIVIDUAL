The Apollo Bay

AUSTRALIA

The Apollo Bay is sculpted coastline originated as billions of tiny marine skeletons collected on the ocean floor, eventually forming limestone formations

They are an offshore collection of limestone stacks along the Great Ocean Road. The cliffs were exposed above the level of the sea when the ocean drained

Each rock has its own background and now there are only 7 left in the rock formation. That is how we end our last day before we went to the airport the next day in the morning and flew back to Malaysia
